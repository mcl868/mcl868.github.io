Playing TicTacToe in R

To play TicTacToe in R then load the library TicTacToe. Use the function "StartGame(Numb = "Single")" to play single player against the computer
An example of playing against the computer in TicTacToe:
>library(TicTacToe)

>StartGame(Numb = "Single")

You are Player Two

Select a field: 5

Select a field: 4

Select a field: 2

Select a field: 9

Game over

Draw

In this particular game you play against the computer and the computer is x and you are o. Player 1 begin the game and it is randomized who begin the game. It is not allowed to choose an occupied field. You pick a field by picking a number. The fields are ordered in sequence. The field top left is one and the field top right is three. The field bottom left is seven and the field bottom right is nine. This game was draw. The eight plots show how the game progress over time. It is also possible to play against your friend by the command "StartGame(Numb = "Twoplayer")".
